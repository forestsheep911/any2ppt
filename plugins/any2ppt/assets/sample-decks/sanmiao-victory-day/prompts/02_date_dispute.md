# 02 date_dispute (sample)

## Meta
- Slide ID: `02_date_dispute`

## Visual Concept
A two-column comparison table. A simple horizontal timeline above the table marks the two dates. Subtle archival paper background. Small icons (surrender document, clock, parade silhouette) used sparingly.

## Composition
- Top: title.
- Below title: small timeline strip with two anchor dates.
- Center: two columns, equal width, with headings on top and 1-2 bullets each.
- Bottom: a single anchor sentence (the slide's takeaway).
