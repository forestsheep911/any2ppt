## Meta
- Slide ID: `00_cover`

- Sample condensed for in-tree teaching. The full prompt the run actually used lives outside the repository.

## Visual Concept
A polished 16:9 lecture title slide. Left half carries the title block; right half a compact collage of three small documentary visual tiles (parade formation, WWII tank silhouette, faded archive texture). Bottom: a small subtitle ribbon.

## Composition
- Left half: title block (title + subtitle), generous left margin.
- Right half: three small documentary tiles in a loose 2x2 collage (one cell intentionally empty for breathing space).
- Bottom edge: subtitle ribbon, horizontal.

## Text to Render (Traditional Chinese)
- Title: **勝利日閱兵：正當性的來源與被透支的歷史遺產**
- Subtitle: 從二戰記憶到當代俄羅斯政治敘事
- Footer: 三喵聊俄國勝利日閱兵

## Style
- Palette: muted red, charcoal, off-white, steel gray.
- Editorial lecture-deck typography; high contrast.
- The collage is support, not the focus.

## Negative
- No logos.
- No identifiable real politicians.
- No gore or weapon close-ups.
- No empty cinematic background.
