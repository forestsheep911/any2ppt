# Storyboard — 三喵聊俄國勝利日閱兵 (sample)

## Deck Meta
- Total slides: 8 (cover + 6 body + closing)
- Aspect ratio: 16:9
- Production mode: image-first
- Budget mode: premium
- Tone: analytical lecture (Traditional Chinese)

---

## 00_cover
- **Title**: 勝利日閱兵：正當性的來源與被透支的歷史遺產
- **Primary job**: Set the lecture frame; promise an argument, not a documentary.
- **Core claim**: 這場閱兵到底在展示什麼？(open with a question)
- **Support points**:
  - Subtitle: 從二戰記憶到當代俄羅斯政治敘事
  - Footer: 三喵聊俄國勝利日閱兵
- **Archetype**: Cover
- **Presenter intent**: Open with a question; do not preview the answer yet.

## 01_core_thesis
- **Title**: 這場閱兵到底在展示什麼？
- **Primary job**: Compress the entire talk into one viewable slide.
- **Core claim**: 它不只是紀念二戰，而是在反覆展示「誰有資格代表勝利與正義」。
- **Support points**:
  - Flow chain: 二戰勝利記憶 → 道德制高點 → 俄羅斯合法性 → 烏克蘭戰爭透支
  - Takeaway: 閱兵越宏大，越需要追問：它在遮住什麼危機？
- **Archetype**: Thesis (with a 4-block flow)
- **Presenter intent**: Pause here; the rest of the deck just unpacks this slide.

## 02_date_dispute
- **Title**: 為什麼俄羅斯堅持 5 月 9 日？
- **Primary job**: Anchor the abstract thesis on one concrete dispute.
- **Core claim**: 日期之爭，本質是勝利敘事的詮釋權。
- **Support points**:
  - Left (5/8): 德國先向盟軍投降；西歐記憶中的「歐戰結束」
  - Right (5/9): 莫斯科時間下的投降儀式；誰定義「打敗法西斯」
- **Archetype**: Comparison (with a small timeline header)
- **Presenter intent**: This is the most "teachable" single slide; spend extra time here.

## 03_complex_history
- **Title**: 蘇聯二戰歷史的兩面
- **Primary job**: Acknowledge the moral complexity before using "anti-fascism" as a tool.
- **Core claim**: 蘇聯既是反法西斯主力，也是早期的戰爭參與者；兩件事都是真的。
- **Support points**:
  - 抵抗：東線戰場巨大犧牲與決定性勝利
  - 共謀：1939 蘇德互不侵犯與隨後的領土瓜分
- **Archetype**: Comparison (two faces)
- **Presenter intent**: Establish nuance before the next slide turns "anti-fascism" into a tool analysis.

## 04_discourse_tool
- **Title**: 「反法西斯」如何變成話語權工具？
- **Primary job**: Reveal the mechanism connecting historical memory and current politics.
- **Core claim**: 誰反對我，誰就可以被貼上「法西斯」標籤。
- **Support points**:
  - 一、佔據道德制高點
  - 二、把反對者重新命名
  - 三、把現實政治包裝成歷史正義
- **Archetype**: Process (3-step diagram)
- **Presenter intent**: This is the deck's analytical core; voice should harden here.

## 05_ritual_shift
- **Title**: 從十月革命到勝利日 — 紀念儀式的轉移
- **Primary job**: Show how the choice of holiday rotates with regime needs.
- **Core claim**: 政權選擇紀念哪一天，就在告訴你它如何想被記得。
- **Support points**:
  - 1917-1991: 十月革命為主節
  - 後蘇聯: 勝利日逐步上升為國族敘事支柱
- **Archetype**: Timeline
- **Presenter intent**: Use a horizontal timeline; speak slowly through dates.

## 06_legacy_depletion
- **Title**: 烏克蘭戰爭如何透支二戰遺產
- **Primary job**: Bring the deck back to the present; cash in the thesis.
- **Core claim**: 高呼「反法西斯」的同時打一場侵略戰，會在世界輿論中折損這份遺產。
- **Support points**:
  - 國際輿論：「反法西斯」標籤被質疑
  - 國內動員：紀念儀式承擔越來越多即時功能
  - 長期成本：道德資本被快速消耗
- **Archetype**: Tension (cause / consequence)
- **Presenter intent**: This is where the deck's argument lands; do not soften the language.

## 07_closing
- **Title**: 越是高喊勝利，越能看見危機
- **Primary job**: Close the loop opened on the cover.
- **Core claim**: 當一個政權越依賴過去的勝利，就越暴露它難以提供新的未來。
- **Support points**:
  - 勝利日閱兵不只是紀念
  - 它是在展示合法性來源
  - 烏克蘭戰爭正在透支這份遺產
- **Archetype**: Closing
- **Presenter intent**: Three short takeaways; do not introduce new ideas.
