# 01 core_thesis (sample)

## Meta
- Slide ID: `01_core_thesis`
- Archetype: Thesis (single-slide compression of the whole talk)

## Visual Concept
A central 4-block flow diagram explaining the chain of cause and consequence. To the right, a small silhouette pair (parade podium + fading medal stack). Title and one-sentence thesis on the upper left.

## Composition
- Top-left: title.
- Below title: one-sentence thesis (large but lighter weight than the title).
- Center: 4 connected blocks left-to-right with arrows.
- Lower-right: small support visual (podium + medals); deliberately small so it does not steal focus from the diagram.

## Text to Render (Traditional Chinese)
- Title: **這場閱兵到底在展示什麼？**
- Thesis: 它不只是紀念二戰，而是在反覆展示「誰有資格代表勝利與正義」。
- Flow blocks (left to right): 二戰勝利記憶 → 道德制高點 → 俄羅斯合法性 → 烏克蘭戰爭透支
- Key takeaway: 閱兵越宏大，越需要追問：它在遮住什麼危機？

## Style
- Off-white background, muted red accent on arrows.
- Block typography clean and uniform.
- Palette consistent with `00_cover`.

## Negative
- No logos, no party symbols.
- No gore.
- No "decorative pseudo-text" — the model must render real Chinese characters.
