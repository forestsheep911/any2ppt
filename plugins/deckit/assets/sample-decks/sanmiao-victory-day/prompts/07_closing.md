# 07 closing (sample)

## Meta
- Slide ID: `07_closing`
- Archetype: Closing

## Visual Concept
A calm, conclusive slide. Large central closing statement; three short takeaway cards underneath; a faint parade silhouette as a low-opacity background accent (visual rhyme with the cover).

## Composition
- Center-top: large closing statement.
- Below it: three small horizontal cards in a row, each carrying one takeaway line.
- Background: very low-opacity parade silhouette behind the cards (decorative, not the focus).
- Bottom-right: small footer.

## Text to Render (Traditional Chinese)
- Title: **越是高喊勝利，越能看見危機**
- Closing statement: 當一個政權越依賴過去的勝利，就越暴露它難以提供新的未來。
- Takeaway 1: 勝利日閱兵不只是紀念
- Takeaway 2: 它是在展示合法性來源
- Takeaway 3: 烏克蘭戰爭正在透支這份遺產
- Footer: 謝謝觀看

## Style
- Same off-white / charcoal / muted red palette as the rest of the deck.
- Closing statement in slightly heavier weight than the takeaways.
